Intro

Welcome to my portfolio, a digital chronicle of my professional journey. Over the past year, I've navigated the dynamic landscape of cloud technology as a freelance Cloud Support Engineer, honing my skills and deepening my understanding of this complex field. My experiences have been diverse and enriching, ranging from troubleshooting intricate cloud issues to designing and implementing robust cloud infrastructures.

This journey has not only fortified my technical acumen but also paved the way for my transition into the realm of DevOps. As a DevOps Engineer, I've embraced the philosophy of unifying software development (Dev) and software operation (Ops), striving to shorten the systems development life cycle and provide continuous delivery with high software quality.

This portfolio is a testament to my growth, a showcase of my expertise, and a glimpse into my passion for innovation and efficiency in the world of cloud computing and DevOps. I invite you to explore my work, my accomplishments, and my journey. Welcome to my world of continuous learning and improvement.

Education

I am a proud alumnus of Chavara CMI Public School, Pala, where I completed my higher secondary education following the CBSE syllabus. My academic journey continued at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam, where I pursued and successfully earned my Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering. These esteemed institutions have provided me with a robust foundation and have shaped my understanding this fast moving world of rising technology.
